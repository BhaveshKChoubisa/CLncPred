        <h3>Getting Started</h3>
        <p>CLnc-Pred is a machine learning tool for predicting long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) in plant genomes. Follow the steps below to use the tool effectively.</p>

        <h3>Step 1: Upload or Paste Sequence</h3>
        <p>You can either:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>Paste a sequence in FASTA format.</li>
            <li>Upload a FASTA file (.fasta, .fa, .txt).</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>Step 2: Click Predict</h3>
        <p>After entering the sequence, click the Predict button to analyze the data.</p>

        <h3>Step 3: View Results</h3>
        <p>The prediction results will be displayed, indicating whether the sequence is a lncRNA or coding RNA.</p>
